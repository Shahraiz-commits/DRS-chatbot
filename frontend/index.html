Privacy Policy

This chatbot service is provided by the University of South Carolina Libraries' Digital Research Services department. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Data Collection and Usage:

We collect:

  • Your voluntary questions and feedback to train and improve the chatbot's responses

We do NOT collect:

  • ANY personal information whatsoever including but not limited to (name, location, email, passwords, etc)

By using this chatbot, you agree to the collection and use of your conversation data as described above. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact the Digital Research Services department.
